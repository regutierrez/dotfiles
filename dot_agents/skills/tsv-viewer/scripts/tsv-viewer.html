TSV Viewer

Drop a .tsv file here or click to browse.

First row is treated as the header. Click a column header to sort (numbers sort numerically), type in the box under a header to filter that column.

Scriptable, no dragging needed: ./tsv-view.sh file.tsv, or open tsv-viewer.html#name=file.tsv&gz=<base64url of gzipped tsv> (also accepts data= for plain base64, or file=<url> when served over http).
